Who We Are

Kindness in Action, Change at Scale

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization, licensed by the Central Government, with its roots in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. We believe in the power of collective action — mobilising volunteers, corporates, and communities to build a more inclusive, compassionate India.

Our mission spans food distribution, quality education, women's empowerment, animal welfare, environmental sustainability, and skill development — reaching the underserved across multiple Indian states.

Section 8 NGO 80G & 12A Certified CSR-1 Registered NITI Aayog ISO 9001:2015

Social Impact

Why Our Work Matters

Every initiative we run is a direct response to a real gap in society. Here's the change we're creating — one community at a time.

🏫

Breaking the Education Barrier

Millions of children in India lack access to basic schooling and digital skills. Project Bachpanshala bridges this gap, empowering children with the tools to build their own futures.

Education for All

No One Should Go Hungry

Food insecurity affects crores of Indians daily. Through Project SEVA, we've ensured over 50,000 hot meals reach the homeless and underprivileged across multiple cities.
